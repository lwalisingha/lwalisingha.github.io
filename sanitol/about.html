Who we are

Sanirub International Pvt Ltd was incorporated on 31 st August 2020 as a limited liability company with the noble idea of developing and manufacturing products to ensure the healthy living of people and preserve the environment precisely. Sanirub International Pvt Ltd is well progressing with developing and manufacturing innovative standard and tailormade chemicals based high quality industrial, home care and personal care products to local and global market in order to solve teething matters in Industries and day today life of households.

In this context, we are using modern product development and manufacturing principles such as Concurrent Engineering, Lean Manufacturing and Total Quality Management with extensive applications of Analytical Chemistry and Chemical Engineering in product development and manufacturing. And We trust that, recruiting employees with right competencies, developing them in right direction and sustainable human resource management practices will develop the business exponentially through delighting all stake holders of the business. Sanirub International Pvt ltd is a GMP certified company by National Medicines Regulatory authority and Practicing ISO 9001 Quality management system with the intention of get certified for the same in near future..
